About me

My name is Robert Thaiyah Kibue and I am a recent graduate having pursued Diploma in Information Technology. My interest in the web development route began when I was in campus and we were told we had to design a project as part of our web development coursework. Although we were taught limited HTML and CSS to go on, a felt a burning passion to venture into the web development world. With that flame, I joined the Odin Project to learn the basics of Web Development and FrontEndMentor to polish my front-end skills.

My hobbies include cooking, taking small walks around the estate and playing video games. I am learning new technologies and plan to improve all my portfolios as I grasp new libraries.
